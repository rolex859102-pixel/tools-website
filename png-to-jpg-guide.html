PNG vs JPG – What’s the Difference?

PNG images have better quality but larger size, while JPG images are compressed and smaller. JPG is best for uploading, sharing and websites.

What is Image Optimization?

Image optimization means reducing image size without losing quality. It helps websites load faster and improves user experience.

Tips for Image Optimization

  • Use JPG instead of PNG when possible
  • Compress images to 50KB or 100KB
  • Resize large images before uploading
  • Avoid unnecessary high resolution
